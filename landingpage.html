Korean study with consistent feedback

Koala Cards is a Korean-only spaced repetition system for English speakers. It focuses on speaking and writing with automatic grading, scheduling, and review workflows that prioritize retention.

The project is in maintenance mode: bug fixes and security updates only.

Current Capabilities

Koala Cards is built for repeatable practice and measurable improvement, with a focus on Korean.

Speaking quizzes

Record responses, transcribe them, and receive automated feedback against the expected Korean phrase.

Writing feedback

Submit writing and get corrections, diffs, and word-level definitions tied to the prompt and context.

Deck and card management

Create, edit, merge, pause, publish, import, and export decks with bulk creation options.

Study assistant

Review recent cards, request explanations, or draft new example cards during study sessions.

Audio support

Term and definition audio are generated via text-to- speech, with playback controls and timing options.

Progress tracking

Track review progress, writing goals, and quiz outcomes through dashboards and export tools.

How It Works

  1. Create a deck with free-form generation, word lists, CSV import, or the first-deck generator.
  2. Study by speaking and let the app transcribe and grade each response.
  3. Practice writing to receive corrections and explanations tied to your decks.
  4. Repeat on schedule as the system prioritizes new, due, and remedial cards.

Language Support

Koala Cards is intentionally focused on Korean (ko) only.

For Developers

Koala Cards is open source and built with Next.js, TypeScript, tRPC, Prisma, and Mantine. It relies on OpenAI for grading and speech services, and Google Cloud for text-to-speech and storage. See the repository for setup details.
